about us

Henan LIMING Heavy Industry Science and Technology Co. LTD which mainly manufacture large and medium-sized crushing and grinding equipments was founded in 1987. Another workshop in Shangjie Industry Park covers 67000 m ². Over the past 30 years, our company adheres to modern scientific management system, precision manufacturing, pioneering and innovation.
